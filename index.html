My Favorite Hobby:

My Idols:

  • Larry Page: Larry Page is the co-founder of Google along with Sergey Brin. In a computer science program at Stanford University, Page met Sergey Brin, and the two started working on a project called BackRub, which eventually became Google. Larry was the CEO of Google for a total of 20 years, and is a residing board member of Alphabet, Google's parent company.
    Larry Page- Wikipedia


  • Bill Gates: William "Bill" Gates is the co-founder of Microsoft along with Paul Allen, who died recently in 2018. Bill Gates was born and raised in Seattle, Washington. He was often interested in computers and technology as a child, and he wrote his first software program at the age of 13. In 2021, he was listed in Forbes as the 4th richest man in the world. From 1995-2009, he was the #1 richest man in the world. Bill Gates is regarded by many the greatest pioneer of modern technology.
    Bill Gates- Wikipedia


  • Tim-Berners Lee: Tim Berners Lee is a British software engineer who founded www: World Wide Web.
    Tim Berners-Lee Wikipedia


  • Steve Jobs: Steve Jobs was a prominent American buisnessman who was adopted as a child. The founder of Apple along with Steve Wozniack, he was known for creating technology with an sleek, premium design, and was praised for producing high-quality products. Apple remains one of the world's Tech Giants even after Jobs' death in 2011.
    Steve Jobs- Wikipedia
